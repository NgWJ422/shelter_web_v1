Frequently Asked Questions

Kidohelp is a platform that collect donations and organize transportation services for kids who cannot afford it. With the generous support of our donors and partners, we ensure that every child has the chance to participate in educational activities and other enriching experiences, regardless of their financial situation.
You can donate by clicking on the "Donate" button and following the on-screen instructions.
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and more.
